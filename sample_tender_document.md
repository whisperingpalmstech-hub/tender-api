# Supply and Implementation of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System

**Issue Date:** January 15, 2026  
**Submission Deadline:** February 28, 2026, 5:00 PM IST  
**Tender Value:** ₹15,00,00,000 (Fifteen Crores Indian Rupees)  
**EMD Amount:** ₹30,00,000 (Thirty Lakhs Indian Rupees)

---

## 1. INTRODUCTION AND BACKGROUND

### 1.1 Purpose
The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) invites sealed bids from reputable Information Technology companies for the supply, customization, implementation, and maintenance of an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System across 25 government offices located in various states of India.

### 1.2 Scope Overview
The selected vendor shall be responsible for:
- Supply of ERP software licenses
- Hardware infrastructure setup
- System customization and configuration
- Data migration from legacy systems
- Training of 500+ end users
- Post-implementation support for 5 years

### 1.3 Project Timeline
| Phase | Duration | Deliverables |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| Phase 1 | 3 months | Requirements gathering, system design |
| Phase 2 | 6 months | Development, customization, testing |
| Phase 3 | 3 months | Deployment, data migration, training |
| Phase 4 | 60 months | Maintenance and support |

## 2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Bidders must meet ALL of the following eligibility requirements:

### 2.1 Legal Requirements
- Must be a registered company under the Companies Act, 2013 or LLP Act, 2008
- Must have valid GST registration
- Must have PAN registration
- Should not be blacklisted by any Central/State Government department

### 2.2 Financial Requirements
- Minimum annual turnover of ₹50 Crores in each of the last 3 financial years (FY 2022-23, 2023-24, 2024-25)
- Positive net worth as per the latest audited balance sheet
- Must submit audited financial statements for the last 3 years

### 2.3 Technical Requirements
- Minimum 10 years of experience in ERP implementation
- Must have successfully completed at least 5 ERP projects of similar nature and scale (value ≥ ₹10 Crores each) in India
- Must have ISO 27001:2013 certification for Information Security
- Must have ISO 9001:2015 certification for Quality Management
- Must have CMM Level 3 or above certification

### 2.4 Manpower Requirements
- Minimum 500 full-time employees on payroll
- Dedicated team of at least 30 certified ERP consultants
- Project Manager with PMP certification and minimum 10 years experience
- At least 5 certified database administrators

## 3. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### 3.1 Functional Modules Required

#### 3.1.1 Finance and Accounting Module
- General Ledger Management
- Accounts Payable and Receivable
- Budget Management and Control
- Fixed Asset Management
- Bank Reconciliation
- GST Compliance and Reporting
- Multi-currency support

#### 3.1.2 Human Resource Management Module
- Employee Master Data Management
- Payroll Processing with statutory compliance
- Leave Management System
- Attendance and Time Tracking
- Performance Appraisal System
- Training and Development Management
- Recruitment and Onboarding

#### 3.1.3 Procurement and Inventory Module
- Vendor Management
- Purchase Requisition and Orders
- Goods Receipt and Inspection
- Inventory Control and Valuation
- Warehouse Management
- GeM (Government e-Marketplace) Integration

#### 3.1.4 Project Management Module
- Project Planning and Scheduling
- Resource Allocation
- Milestone Tracking
- Budget vs Actual Analysis
- Risk Management
- Document Management

### 3.2 Non-Functional Requirements

#### 3.2.1 Performance Requirements
- System response time should be less than 3 seconds for 95% of transactions
- Must support 1000+ concurrent users
- Database should handle minimum 10 TB of data
- System availability of 99.9% (excluding planned maintenance)

#### 3.2.2 Security Requirements
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) support
- End-to-end encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Audit trail for all transactions
- Compliance with IT Act 2000 and subsequent amendments
- Data residency within India

#### 3.2.3 Integration Requirements
- Integration with Aadhaar-based authentication
- Integration with PFMS (Public Financial Management System)
- Integration with NIC email services
- REST API support for third-party integrations
- Single Sign-On (SSO) capability

#### 3.2.4 Technology Stack Requirements
- Web-based application accessible via modern browsers
- Mobile application for Android and iOS
- Database: Oracle 19c or PostgreSQL 14+
- Application Server: Apache Tomcat or similar
- Cloud-compatible architecture (for future migration)
